combat system:
the player and enemy confront, whoever has the highest speed goes first
that entity attacks and deals damage equal to attack power
the second highest goes next and attacks
scenario ends when either entity reaches 0 hp
if enemy hp reaches 0, player continues
	when scenario ends, the player receives xp and the story continues
if player hp reaches 0, game over
	if the player wants to continue, they can make a new character and begin the story again
	if not, the program closes
